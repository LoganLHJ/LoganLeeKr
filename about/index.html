Overview

I am a data engineer specializing in omics-scale biomedical data, working at the intersection of healthcare innovation, AI, and bioinformatics. My core expertise lies in large-scale genome data processing, multi-omics integration, and biological interpretation, including leading and contributing to nationwide whole-genome sequencing (WGS) initiatives in the Korean population. I develop scalable research data platforms that enable clinicians and scientists to extract meaningful insights from complex biological datasets, supporting precision-medicine–driven decision making.

Building on collaborative work across academia and industry, I aim to extend my contributions beyond genomic analytics toward protein-level modeling and computational frameworks that can inform drug discovery. By connecting population-scale omics data with mechanistic understanding at the molecular level, I seek to advance the full pipeline—from data engineering to actionable therapeutic innovation.
