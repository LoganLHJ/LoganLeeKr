Overview

I am a data engineer and bioinformatics analyst working at the intersection of biomedical data, large-scale genomics, and computational biology. My work focuses on building scalable data pipelines, processing population-scale omics datasets, and enabling clinically meaningful insights through computational analysis.

I am particularly interested in translating raw genomic and multi-omics data into interpretable biological signals that support precision medicine. By combining data engineering principles with biological reasoning, I aim to bridge the gap between large clinical datasets and actionable discoveries in healthcare and therapeutic development.
